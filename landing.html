About Vizag Street Foods

Discover the flavors of Visakhapatnam's streets. From savory snacks to mouth-watering sweets, explore the diverse range of street foods Vizag has to offer.

Our Menu

Dosa

A popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter, served with chutney and sambar.

Pani Puri

A beloved street food consisting of hollow puri filled with spicy water, tamarind chutney, potatoes, and chickpeas.
